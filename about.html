About

Welcome to Abhi Interiors, where creativity meets craftsmanship. Founded with a passion for transforming spaces into beautiful and functional environments, we are proud to carry forward a legacy of excellence in interior design. Abhi Interiors is led by Maneesh Vishwakarma, continuing a family tradition rooted in skill, dedication, and vision. Inspired by my father, Rajendra Vishwakarma, a veteran with over 30 years of experience in the interior design industry, I have grown up surrounded by the art of design and the values of hard work, quality, and client satisfaction. At Abhi Interiors, we blend traditional expertise with modern aesthetics. Whether it's a residential makeover or a commercial transformation, our designs are customized to reflect your personality, lifestyle, and needs. From concept to completion, we ensure every detail is handled with care and precision.
